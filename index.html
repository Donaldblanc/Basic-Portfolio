About Me


Donald Blanc is a Brooklyn native from Crown Heights. He received a degree from Rochester Institute of Technology. He is passionate about technology and how it can better use it as tool for improved productivity. In his free time, he runs a small basketball league with his colleagues, runs marathons and learning about where the industry is headed.

Donald takes complex technical ideas and distills them into user-friendly visuals for management and none technical audiences. He as an education in electrical engineering and information technology. He is a modern magician when it comes to network technologies and currently sharpening his development skills to potentially land a role into Devops.

His portfolio showcases his ability to write fully functional and efficient code without sacrificing the attractiveness of user interface and experience. He is ready and open for new development and design opportunities and have plenty of room up his sleeve to experiment and learn new technologies.
